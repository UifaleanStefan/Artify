Ancient Worlds pack – restore the 15 correct images
====================================================

The images in this folder were overwritten by the Evolution Portraits set.
Git does not have the originals. To restore:

1. Download the 15 images below (in this order).
2. Put ONLY these 15 files in your Downloads folder. Oldest file = 01, newest = 15.
   (Easiest: download in the order listed, then run the script.)
3. From project root run:
   python scripts/setup_ancient_worlds_pack.py

Required order (01 = first → 15 = last):

 01  Nebamun Hunting in the Marshes (Ancient Egypt)
     e.g. British Museum / Wikimedia: Nebamun hunting in the marshes

 02  Akhenaten and Nefertiti with their Children (Egypt, Amarna)

 03  Book of the Dead of Hunefer (Egypt)

 04  Tomb of Ramesses I Wall Paintings (Egypt)

 05  Achilles and Ajax Playing Dice Amphora (Greece)

 06  The Berlin Painter Amphora (Greece)

 07  The Francois Vase (Greece)

 08  Alexander Mosaic (Rome)

 09  Villa of Livia Garden Room (Rome)

 10  Pompeii Fresco of Bacchus (Rome)

 11  Fayum Mummy Portraits (Roman Egypt)

 12  Standard of Ur (Mesopotamia)

 13  Ishtar Gate Reliefs (Babylon)

 14  Ajanta Cave Paintings (India)

 15  Han Dynasty Silk Paintings (Ancient China)

Search on Wikimedia Commons or museum sites for public-domain images.
Save as JPG when possible so filenames are ancient-worlds-01.jpg … ancient-worlds-15.jpg.
